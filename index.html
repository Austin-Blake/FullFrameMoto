Full Framed Moto "WCP"

Born and raised in the backwoods of Indiana, photographer Blake Hall quickly grew an affinity for all things with an engine. Becoming deeply embedded in motorcycle culture, he saw a need for quality photography, for the community & their motorcycles. Full Frame Moto "WCP" exists to fill that void in photography, crafted with a passion.

Founder / Photographer

Blake Hall

Union IronWorker, served in the US Armed Forces, City Police Officer, Photographer, and also a Web Develper. Blake has worn many hats in his life time, but his unique artistic design, and high quality Products is where he truly shines.
