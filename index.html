Full Framed Moto "WCP"

Born and raised in the backwoods of Indiana, photographer Blake Hall quickly grew an affinity for all things with an engine. Becoming deeply embedded in motorcycle culture, he saw a need for quality, photography with a lifestyle vibe, for the community & their motorcycles. Full Frame Moto "WCP" exists to fill that void in photography, crafted with a passion.
